Nature of Enterprise Computing — CA2

This project analyses customer shopping behaviour using descriptive statistics, visualisations, and a basic machine learning model.

View Analysis View ML Model
Quick Summary
  • Dataset: Shopping behaviour (Kaggle)
  • Rows: 3900
  • Numeric variables: Age, Purchase Amount, Review Rating
  • ML model: Logistic Regression predicting Subscription Status
  • Model accuracy: ~86%

Key Findings

Spending

Purchase amounts range from $20–$100, with an average around $60.

Payments & Sizes

PayPal is the most common payment method, and Size M is most frequently purchased.

Subscriptions

Most customers are non-subscribers (0), while a smaller share are subscribers (1).
